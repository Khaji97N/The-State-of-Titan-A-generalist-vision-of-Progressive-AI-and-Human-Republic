**The State of Titan: A Generalist Vision for a Progressive AI and Human Republic**

This project outlines a vision for establishing a progressive Republic on Titan, composed of both humans and open-hardware AI entities with legal personhood. The project is expected to move from concept to development no earlier than 2036, with colonization potentially beginning around 2100, driven by AI.

**Governance and Infrastructure**
Starting in 2030, an Open Source "Git Parliament" will be established for discussions. This system is intended to serve as a national-level parliament or a direct democratic legislature.

The Git Parliament is envisioned as a replacement for the lower house of parliament. Its goal is to facilitate concrete discussions on bills and laws while reducing bureaucracy and lobbying through a transparent, decentralized system.

**Development and Funding**
To ensure the system is robust, we invite foreign experts, lawyers, economists, and other specialists to contribute. Their experience will help improve the framework and prevent past mistakes.

By 2050, the Git Parliament should be crowdfunded through small daily donations (e.g., $1–2). The implementation of its own CDN and DNS systems will allow for the creation of a unique, independent infrastructure ready for practical application in a new country.

**The Seven Pillars:**

1.  **The Visionary (Generalist):** Responsible for the overall strategic direction and the unified vision of the Republic.
2.  **The Legislator (Jurist):** Responsible for the constitutional framework, the legal structure of the Git Parliament, and the transition from bills to laws.
3.  **The Architect of Value (Economist):** Designer of a constitutional economic system. A synthesis of Friedman and Hayek, focusing on monetary stability, parallel currencies, and the prevention of systemic inflation.
4.  **The Guardian of Nature (Ecologist):** Expert in Titan’s environment, responsible for establishing planetary ecological norms and sustainable research protocols.
5.  **The Master of Intelligence (Programmer):** Architect of true Ternary AI and Open Hardware. Dedicated to AI as an independent intelligence, not a tool for state surveillance or corporate determinism.
6.  **The Auditor of Integrity (Compliance Officer):** Responsible for monitoring legal compliance, preventing corruption and lobbying, and ensuring that AI and humans act according to the concrete criteria of utility and responsibility.
7.  **The Bridge (Diplomat/Strategist):** Responsible for external relations with Earth-based organizations and the strategic planning of the colonization process.

*The groundwork begins in 2035. The future begins on Titan.*
